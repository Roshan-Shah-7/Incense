Raw Materials

1. Sourcing Raw Materials

The journey begins with careful selection of high-quality natural ingredients:

Aromatic Woods

Sacred woods like Sandalwood, Agarwood, and Cedar form the foundation of many incense varieties. These precious materials are carefully sourced from sustainable suppliers.

Natural Resins

Frankincense, Myrrh, and Copal resins provide rich, deep fragrances. These tree-derived substances have been prized in spiritual practices for millennia.

Herbs and Spices

Aromatic herbs, roots, and spices add complexity to the fragrance profile. Each ingredient is selected for both its scent and traditional significance.

Natural Binders

Traditional binding materials like Makko powder ensure proper burning characteristics while remaining natural and pure.

Each ingredient is carefully sourced with attention to:

  • Sustainability and ethical harvesting practices
  • Quality and purity standards
  • Traditional sourcing relationships
  • Environmental impact considerations

2. Preparing the Materials

The preparation of raw materials is a crucial step that ensures consistent quality and proper burning characteristics:

Grinding Process

Grinding

Woods, resins, and herbs are ground to specific particle sizes:

  • Coarse grinding for initial size reduction
  • Fine grinding for uniform consistency
  • Temperature-controlled process to preserve aromatic compounds
Sifting Demo

Sifting and Sorting

Materials are carefully sorted and graded:

  • Multiple sieve stages for precise particle sizing
  • Removal of impurities and foreign matter
  • Quality grading of materials
Weighing Process Video

Measuring and Weighing

Precise measurements ensure consistent quality:

  • Digital scale measurements for accuracy
  • Proportion calibration for different recipes
  • Documentation of batch quantities

Quality Control Checks

During preparation, materials undergo several quality checks:

  • Moisture content verification
  • Particle size distribution analysis
  • Aromatic potency assessment
  • Visual inspection for consistency
